# Showcase

This webpage is a gallery of the games created by students during Coding Club.  
Each project was designed and programmed by the students themselves using the skills they developed over the past six weeks.

## How to Run the Programs

1. Press the black  **Play ▶ button** to start the game.  
1. Read the story and make decisions as the text unfolds — your choices will shape what happens next!
