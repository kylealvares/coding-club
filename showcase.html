Showcase

This webpage is a gallery of the “Choose Your Own Adventure” games created by students during Coding Club.
Each project was designed and programmed by the students themselves using the skills they developed over the past six weeks.

How to Run the Programs

  1. Click on any story title in the menu to the right to open that story.
  2. Press the black Play ▶ button to start the game.
  3. Read the story and make decisions as the text unfolds — your choices will shape what happens next!
